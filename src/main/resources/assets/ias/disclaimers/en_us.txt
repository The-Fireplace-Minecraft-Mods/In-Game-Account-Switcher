Your account data (basically the LOGIN and the PASSWORD) are STORED IN THIS FOLDER.

> If you DID SEND this folder to anyone:
1. Change your Microsoft password here: https://account.microsoft.com/security
2. Revoke the access from "In-Game Account Switcher" here: https://account.live.com/consent/manage
3. Do not use this mod for at least a month.

> About
In-Game Account Switcher is a mod for Minecraft that allows you to change
your logged in account in-game, without restarting Minecraft.
